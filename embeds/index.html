How to Create High-Quality Embeds (Not GIFs) (With Sound on Mobile)

Welcome back to another tutorial, a rather simple one.

I am going to show you how to embed videos in various places like Discord.

For this I will mainly focus on what happens in Discord.
The videos will show up as embeds that can be favorited, but they are not GIFs.

1. Find your video

Choose the video you want to make an embed.

2. Get into an Imgur account

Head over to https://imgur.com/ and either create or log into an account.

3. Post the video on Imgur

The max length is 3 minutes. Keep the audio while posting.

4. Copy its link

Imgur will provide a link. Copy it.

5. Send the link

If the site/app supports it, the video will embed. On Discord, it will show as a high-quality embed that you can add to favorites.
